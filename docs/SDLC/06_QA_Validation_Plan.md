# Quality Assurance (QA) & Validation Plan

* **Target Length:** 15 to 20 pages
* **Scope and Structural Blueprint:** Establishes test strategies, traceability matrices, and manual verification checklists.
* **Core Sections:**
  * **Testing Strategy & Methodology:** Unit, integration, system, regression, and UAT testing.
  * **Requirements Traceability Matrix (RTM):** Bidirectional matrix mapping PRD requirements to test cases.
  * **Manual Verification Checklists:** Exhaustive protocols covering Study Design, Versioning Parity, EDC component behaviors, and Query Management.
* **Standards Alignment:** IEC 62304 and ISO 14155:2020.

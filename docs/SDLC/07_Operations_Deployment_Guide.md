# Operations & Deployment Guide

* **Target Length:** 8 to 12 pages
* **Scope and Structural Blueprint:** Guides system deployment, environment promotion, and configuration management.
* **Core Sections:**
  * **Environment Promotion & Infrastructure:** CI/CD pipelines, staging environments, and infrastructure-as-code.
  * **Configuration Management:** Sponsor-specific settings and tenant-level provisioning.
  * **Migration & Rollback Procedures:** Automated database migrations and version rollback mechanisms.
  * **Operational Monitoring:** System health checks, log aggregation, and incident response.
* **Standards Alignment:** ISO/IEC 27001 and IEC 62304.
